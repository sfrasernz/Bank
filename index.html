Lots grid

Type counts into the grid. Any non-empty number “locks in” automatically (green outline). Enter / Tab moves forward; Shift+Enter / Shift+Tab moves backward.
Notes are treated as mixed across lots for bundling. The app computes the mixed “unbundled bank pile” as: (sum of all lot counts) minus (bundles already extracted).
